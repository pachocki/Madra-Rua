Let Me
Introduce
myself

Hey my name is Iza. I have been breeding Irish Setters for 10 years, but I have been associated with this breed for much longer. My interest in dogs began when I was a child, we always had dogs in our house and I loved to play with them, go for a walk and learn new things. When I was a teenager I started to read books about dogs and deepened myself to the subject of breeds, training, behavior and breeding. I improved this knowledge and in 2011 I decided to start my Irish Setter kennel. My first dogs were Carlos and Missy. These dogs are multiple winners at international and national shows. From year to year I develop my kennel and together with my dogs I achieve new successes.
